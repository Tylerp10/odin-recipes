A Bowl of Cereal

Description

What better way to start your morning than with a fast yet delicious bowl of Vector cereal. A high protein bowl of cereal to kick off your day before some intense training.

Ingredients

  • Vector cereal
  • 250ml of almond milk
  • 1 teaspoon of chia seeds (for the extra fiber)

Instructions

  1. Grab appropriate sized bowl and begin pouring Vector cereal until desired amount
  2. Add almond milk to the mix
  3. Sprinkle in some chia seeds to top it off
  4. Stir it up and enjoy!
